Our Project

Purpose

  1. We want people to learn more about electric cars, as they are the vehicles of tomorrow and are more environmentally friendly than gasoline cars.
  2. We also want to help people make informed decisions when choosing which electric car to buy.

Audience

  1. People interested in electric cars and their advantages, as well as those wanting to buy electric cars.

Goals

  1. Have enough information about electric cars on our website so that people are well informed about electric car companies not in the mainstream.
  2. We also want to help people make informed decisions if they are wanting to buy a car from any of the companies listed on our website.
  3. Apply a variety of services for users to play with - Car builder, random fact generator, live rankings, car game
